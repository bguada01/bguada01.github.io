About me

I am Italian with East African backgrounds; I was born in Puglia (Apulia), a beautiful region of southern Italy overlooking the sea. Before moving to the UK I lived in Spain, in Barcelona precisely. It was one of the most beautiful life experiences ever.

I speak three languages, but I would like to speak many more. I am actually learning Japanese and planning to move to Osaka very soon.

Although I am not very familiar with technology, I believe in creativity. I am sure that the module will be very stimulating for me and I hope it will help develop new skills. I am glad to be able to take part to DMDD module.
